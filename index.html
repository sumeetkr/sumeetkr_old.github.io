Background

I am currently working as a founder at Social AI transforming some of my research into products. I have completed my PhD in Social Media Analytics from the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. Before joininig CMU, I have worked at various companies (including Symantec , Talentica , and Intergraph ) as a Software Engineer.

My research interests lie at the intersection of AI and society. My current focus is to apply deep-learning tools in online social-networks research e.g. learing stance of social-media users, and detecting fake news. You can find my CV here and links to some of my recent papers in the publications section. My open-source projects are on Github.
